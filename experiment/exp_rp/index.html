Instructions

In this experiment, you will be reading and rating some English sentences and answering questions about the sentences afterwards. One sentence will appear on each slide, and your job is to rate how acceptable the given sentence sounds and answer comprehension questions based on information in the given sentences.

When rating the sentences, please use your intuition rather than school grammar.

The sentence "Who did you go to school with?" is considered bad according to school grammar (because of the stranded preposition with). However, most English speakers find it totally acceptable. If you think a sentence is acceptable, you should give it a high rating regardless of what you learned in school.

The questions following each sentence will ask about some information in the sentence. It will be a multiple choice question and please try your best to answer.
